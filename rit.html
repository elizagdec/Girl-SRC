Rochester Institute of Technology

Rochester Institute of Technology (RIT) is a technical school in a suburb of Rochester, NY. RIT includes nine colleges, including the National Technical Institute for the Deaf. In addition to computer science and engineering, the university also offers majors in fine arts and imaging sciences.
