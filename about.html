About

GirlSRC is dedicated to creating a supportive and informational environment for girls interested in pursuing an education in the STEM fields. Through selecting the desired region, our tools enable you to discover information regarding prominent STEM colleges in that area, including testimonies from female students about their experience there. Here at GirlSRC, we provide Support for girls in STEM from girls in STEM.
